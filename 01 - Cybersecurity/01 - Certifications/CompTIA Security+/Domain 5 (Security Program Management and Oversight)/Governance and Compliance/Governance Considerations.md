## Regulatory Considerations
These regulations can cover a wide range of areas, from data protection and privacy to environmental standards and labor laws.
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
Non-compliance with these regulations can result in severe penalties, including fines, sanctions, and damage to the organization's reputation.
## Legal Considerations
Closely tied to regulatory considerations, but they also encompass other areas such as contract law, intellectual property, and corporate law.
- Minimum Wage
- Overtime
- Health and Safety
- Anti-Discrimination
- Employee Benefits
## Industry Considerations
The specific standards and practices that are prevalent in a particular industry.
- Agile Methodologies
Organizations that fails to adhere to these industry standards may find themselves at a competitive disadvantage or facing criticism from stakeholders.
## Geographical Considerations
Can be local, regional, national or global regulations.
- **Local Considerations**: Local ordinance in a city of zoning laws.
- **Regional Considerations**: Imposed by a state or province.
- **National Considerations**: Laws at the national level.
- **Global Considerations**: Can be the GDPR. Even with is based outside of the EU, if it collects or process the data of EU citizens, it must comply with the GDPR.
---
Organizations must comply with a myriad of regulations, which can vary depending on the industry and location.
