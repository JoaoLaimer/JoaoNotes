#### Chip Washing
Repackaging the contents of a microchip with a less expensive one or one with embedded malware.

### CHIPS Act (2002)
U.S federal statute that provide roughly $280 billion in new funding to boost research and manufacturing of semiconductors inside the U.S, also includes $39 billion in subsidies for chip manufacturing on US soil, along with 25% investment tax credit, and another $13 billion for semiconductors research and workforce training.
