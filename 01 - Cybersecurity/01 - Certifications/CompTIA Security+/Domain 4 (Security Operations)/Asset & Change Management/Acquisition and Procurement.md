## Acquisition
Process of obtaining goods and services
## Procurement
Encompasses the full process of a acquiring goods and services, including all preceding steps
## Company Credit Card
Organization often permit employees to use company credit cards for small purchases on the organization's behalf.
## Individual Purchase
An individual purchase entails employees buying on the company's behalf and later seeking reimbursement from their employer.
## Purchase Order
Formal document issued by the purchasing department that authorizes a specific purchase, usually a larger purchase.

An approval process for purchases ensures alignment with the company's goals and needs.
