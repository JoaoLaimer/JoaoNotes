## [[Change Management]]
Structured approach to transitioning individuals, teams, and organizations from a current state to a desired future state.
Every change should undergo a strict approval process while considering a lot of different aspects
- Change advisory board's insights.
- Ownership of change
- Stakeholder involvement
- Thorough impact analysis
### [[Change Management Processes]]
- Schedule maintenance windows
- Possess a well-thought-out backout plan
- Consistently test results after any change implementation
### [[Technical Implication of Changes]]
- Allow lists and Deny lists
- Understanding the nuances of restricted activities
- Managing downtime efficiently
- Handling service or application restarts
