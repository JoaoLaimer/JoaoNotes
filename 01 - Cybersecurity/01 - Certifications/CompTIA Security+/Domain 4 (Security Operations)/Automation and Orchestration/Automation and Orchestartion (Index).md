## Automation 
Automatic execution of tasks without manual involvement
## Orchestration 
Coordination of the automated tasks for a specific outcome or workflow.
## Security, Orchestration, Automation and Response (SOAR)
Means Security Orchestration, Automation, and Response. SOAR's automation capabilities make it serve in incident response primarily.
SOAR integrates with SIEM and creates a next-generation SIEM.
Incident response may be performed immediately to provision resources.
## Playbook
Checklist of actions for specific incident responses.
## Runbook
Automated versions of playbooks with human interaction points.
