## Brute Force Attack
Involves trying every possible combination of characters until the correct password is found.
## Dictionary Attack
Using a list of commonly sued passwords and trying them all.
## Password Spraying
A form of brute force attack that involves trying a small number of commonly used passwords against a large number of usernames or accounts.
Password spraying attacks avoid triggering account lockouts from too many failed login attempts on one account.
## Hybrid Attack
Blends brute force and dictionary techniques by using common password with variations, such as adding numbers or special characters.
